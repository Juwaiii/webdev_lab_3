My Hobby

I do have to say i have lots of hobby.

I really love sleeping because it’s just the best way to recharge and reset. After a long day, there’s nothing better than crawling into bed and finally letting go of all the stress. Sleep gives my body a chance to recover and boosts my energy, so I wake up feeling refreshed and ready to tackle the day. It’s also when my mind sorts things out, organizing memories and just giving me a mental reset. Plus, it’s the ultimate cozy time—just being wrapped up in blankets, feeling warm and relaxed. Honestly, sleep is like a mini escape, and I love how much better I feel after a good night’s rest.

I love reading because it’s like stepping into a different world, where anything can happen. When I get lost in a good book, I forget about everything around me and feel like I’m actually living in the story. It’s such a great way to unwind and let my imagination run wild, picturing all the scenes and characters in my mind. Reading also teaches me new things, makes me think in different ways, and lets me see life from perspectives I wouldn’t otherwise experience. Plus, there’s something really comforting about just curling up with a book and getting wrapped up in a story—it’s a little escape from everyday life.

I love dancing. Im a korean pop fan and for as long as i can remember when ever i listen to their music my body can stop moving. I also find it annoying to not knowing the dance move so i love learning it and dance to the music.
